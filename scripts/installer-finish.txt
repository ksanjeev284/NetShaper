NetShaper is installed.

Next steps
----------
1. Launch NetShaper (checkbox below, or Start Menu)
2. Wait 1–2 seconds on Live traffic for rates to warm up
3. Limit or block an app, then click Apply all

Tips
----
• Run elevated (UAC) for WFP firewall, QoS limits, and best rates
• Soft shaper mode is the default (good balance)
• Packet mode needs WinDivert (optional)
• Uninstall anytime via Apps & Features → NetShaper

CLI (if you enabled PATH)
-------------------------
  NetShaper.Cli.exe sample 2
  NetShaper.Cli.exe limit chrome 500
  NetShaper.Cli.exe apply-all --persist
